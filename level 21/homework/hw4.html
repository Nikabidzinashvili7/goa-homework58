Information about the War

The Falklands War was a conflict between Argentina and the United Kingdom in 1982. The war was about control of the Falkland Islands, which are in the South Atlantic Ocean.

On April 2, 1982, Argentina invaded the islands, claiming they belonged to them. But the United Kingdom had controlled the islands since 1833 and decided to fight back.

The British sent a military force to take back the islands. There were battles on land, at sea, and in the air. After about 10 weeks of fighting, British forces defeated the Argentinians.

On June 14, 1982, the war ended with a British victory. The Falkland Islands stayed under British control.
