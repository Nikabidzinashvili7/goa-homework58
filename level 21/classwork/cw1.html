habits

Cats are mysterious creatures who love long naps and quiet corners. They scratch to mark territory and knead soft surfaces when they’re feeling cozy and content. A purring cat is usually happy, but sometimes it’s their way of soothing themselves. Sudden bursts of energy, or “zoomies,” come from their playful, hunting instincts. They might gift you a toy—or even a bug—as a sign of affection. Every flick of their tail or rub against your leg is a silent way of saying, “You’re part of my world.”
